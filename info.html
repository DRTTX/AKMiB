Interactive Study Tools

Offers a digital whiteboard feature where students can draw, write, and annotate concepts using different pen types, colors, and sizes. Enable students to highlight text passages, take inline notes, and create summaries within study materials. Enable collaborative study sessions where students can simultaneously interact with pens, texts, and shapes during group discussions or projects.

Templates Tool

A comprehensive library categorizing templates based on subjects, projects, assignments, or specific academic needs. Lab Report Templates: Structured formats for scientific lab reports with sections for hypothesis, methodology, results, and analysis.

Sticky Notes/Notes

Allows users to create multiple digital sticky notes that can be customized in terms of color, size, and font. System allowing users to categorize notes by subject, topic, or priority using tags or labels. Allows users to share individual notes or entire sets of notes with collaborators or study groups for group discussions.

User Profile/Online Meetings

Allows students to create profiles with basic information such as name, profile picture, academic year, interests, and areas of study. Includes sections for users to highlight their skills, certifications, academic achievements, and extracurricular activities. Includes features for screen sharing, collaborative whiteboards, and document sharing during meetings. Offers a dashboard showcasing users' recent activities, upcoming meetings, or events relevant to their connections.
